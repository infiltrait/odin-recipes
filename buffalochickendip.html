Buffalo Chicken Dip

Fast Dip Recipe

The quickest dip recipe with spicy flavors that will keep everyone coming back for more.

CAUTION: have water nearby!

Ingredients

  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ¼ cup blue cheese
  • 1 tablespoon Trappey's Red Devil™ Cayenne Pepper Sauce
  • 1 (4.25 ounce) can Underwood® White Meat Chicken Spread

Recipe Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients until well blended.
  2. Serve with assorted vegetables or crackers.
  3. If preferred served hot, bake in oven at 350 degrees F until bubbling.
